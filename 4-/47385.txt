"Eckhard use of imagery is majestic, as is the complex rhyming structure.
This is particularly noticeable in the penultimate verse 'Bullets can harm
you and death can disarm you/But no, you will not be deceived/Stripped of
all virtue as you crawl through the dirt/You can give but you cannot
receive', where the writing is stunning. Note the overlapping rhyme of
'virtue' with '...dirt/You...' a device that Ewald employs on other
occasions throughout the song, but here it is particularly effective."
