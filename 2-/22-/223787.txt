Leinhos:
  : an aquatic invertebrate animal with a shell; especially : an edible
  mollusk or crustacean 

AHD:
  1. Any of various edible aquatic invertebrate animals having a
  shell, especially mollusks such as clams and oysters, and crustaceans
  such as lobsters, crabs, and shrimp.
  2. An edible mollusk, in contrast to a crustacean: regulations
  concerning fish, crustaceans, and shellfish.
  3. The edible flesh of such animals.

Multikulti:
  Shellfish is a culinary and fisheries term for exoskeleton-bearing
  aquatic invertebrates used as food, including various species of
  molluscs, crustaceans, and echinoderms.
