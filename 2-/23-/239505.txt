Indeed. Already the 1912 revision of the Luther translation of the Bible
considers "Schächer" obsolete enough to no longer use it, even though it often
uses archaisms that were obsolescent or obsolete as early as 1912.  And, gee,
not even the 1545 contains this word. In Mt.27:38, we have:

  Andrees 1545: Mörder
  Andrees 1912: Mörder
  Andrees 1984: Räuber
  Radschlag Bibel: Verbrecher
  AmLimit: Räuber
