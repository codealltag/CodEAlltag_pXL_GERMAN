The seasons in West-Untermurbach were written by Ottmar Zurstrassen, originally an 
author in the GDR, but had been allowed to move to West Germany in the 
late 70s. (best known work "Orlando der Lügner")

The seasons in East Untermurbach were written by the east-German author Friedhelm 
Rengers ("Die neuen Leiden des jungen W.", "Die Legende von Claus und 
Nelly").

Both were notable authors in the history of newer German literature.
