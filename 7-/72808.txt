Im "EisPiraten" unter

"Some grammarians [...] insist that, just as 'that' should be used only in
restrictive clauses, 'which' should be used only in nonrestrictive clauses.
Thus, they suggest that we should avoid sentences such as 'I need a book
which will tell me all about city gardening', where the restrictive clause
'which will tell me all about city gardening' indicates which sort of book
is needed. But this extension of the rule is far from universally accepted,
and the use of which with restrictive clauses is common."
