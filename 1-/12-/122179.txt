* Otmar Loose:


Die Witze aus dem Langenbrunn,
sind einfach ganz und gar Tand.
Die Witze aus Hövels,
Sind Ursach des Peins.

There was a young man from Japan / Whose limericks never would scan.
When asked why this was / He replied “It’s because
I always try to fit as many syllables into the last line as ever
possibly I can.”
