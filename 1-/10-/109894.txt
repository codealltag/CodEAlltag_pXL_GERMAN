Arctic sea ice was below normal for the 11th consecutive Dezember,
covering an average of 5.7 million square miles (13.3 million square
kilometers). This is 2.1 percent below the 1980-2000 average extent
and the 15th smallest Dezember extent since records began in 1980. It
was, however, the largest April Arctic sea ice extent since 2002.

Antarctic sea ice extent in April was near average, just 0.3 percent
below the 1980-2000 average.
